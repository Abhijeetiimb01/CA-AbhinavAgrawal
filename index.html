About Me

I am a qualified chartered accountant and have been an active member of ICAI since 2011. I have an immense passion towards my profession and want to do more for my fellow members. In 2013, I founded a welfare society, "Eagle - The Power of Youth," alongside esteemed colleagues to shape young CA members, and I currently act as its Vice President.

Driven by a sheer commitment to serve the CA fraternity on a larger scale, I successfully ran for the NIRC of ICAI elections. I was elected as Vice-Chairman (2022-23), Secretary (2023-24), and I currently hold the office of the Chairman of NIRC of ICAI for the 2024-25 term.

With rigorous determination, pure hard work, and the blessings of my fellow members, I truly believe together we can build a better future for our profession. I am always available to offer a helping hand—if you need any support or assistance, please reach out.
